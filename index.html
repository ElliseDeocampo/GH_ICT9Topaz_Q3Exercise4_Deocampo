Serena Williams
 Serena Williams is titled one of the greatest tennis players of all time. She was ranked #1 in singles Women's Tennis Association for 319 weeks. $260 Million

Mia Hamm
 Mia Hamm is considered the best female football player in history. She is a two-time Olympic gold medalist and a two-time FIFA Women's World Cup champion. $10 Million

Simone Biles
 Simone Biles is an American gymnast with seven Olympic medals. She set a new record for most gold medals in gymnastics at a single Olympic Games. $16 Million

Florence Griffith Joyner
 Florence Griffith Joyner or "FloJo" was an American Track and Field athlete. She broke two Olympic records in the Seoul Olympic Games. $3-5 Million

Sue Bird
 Sue Bird, who played with the Seattle Storm, is recognized as one of the greatest WNBA players in history. $8 Million
